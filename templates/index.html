Chronic Disease Predictor

An intelligent system to assist in early detection of chronic diseases.

This tool is developed as part of our final year project to help in the early prediction of chronic diseases using clinical data. It utilizes intelligent algorithms to analyze patient health parameters and generate insights that may assist healthcare providers in diagnosis. Our aim is to contribute towards preventive healthcare and enable timely intervention.

We believe that technology can play a crucial role in bridging the gap between data and medical decisions. This application is a step toward building a smarter, more accessible healthcare future.
